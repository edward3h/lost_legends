Codex: Lost Legends

In the spirit of the official Legends publications, this document provides updated rules for older models which no longer appear in existing sources.

This document is fan created and entirely unofficial.

Some datasheets may include rules which appear in the relevant faction Codex - they are not repeated here.

4Lord Solar Macharius
Name M WS BS S T W A Ld Sv Lord Solar Macharius 6" 2+ 3+ 3 3 5 3 10 4+
Lord Solar Macharius is a single model armed with a Master-Crafted Bolt Pistol, the Solar Staff and the Lion's Teeth. Only one of this model may be included in your army.
Weapon Range Type S AP D Abilities Master-Crafted Bolt Pistol 12" Pistol 1 4 -1 2 - Solar Staff Melee Melee +2 -1 2 - Lion's Teeth Melee Melee User 0 1 Each time the bearer fights, it can make 3 additional attacks with this weapon. Abilities
Jealous Command: Macharius must be the Warlord of an army he is included in. He has the Grand Strategist Warlord Trait. In addition he may only be included in an army if every unit in the army has the Astra Militarum, Space Wolves or Inquisition keywords.
Impetuous: Macharius may make a Heroic Intervention if he is within 6" of an enemy unit at the end of the enemy Charge phase, and may move up to 6" instead of 3" when doing so. In addition, while Macharius is within 1" of an enemy unit, add 1 to the Attacks characteristic of friendly Astra Militarum Infantry units within 6".
Inspiring Leader: Astra Militarum units within 12" of Macharius may use his Leadership instead of their own. In addition, Macharius counts as belonging to the <Regiment> of the detachment he is in.
Helm of Macharius: grants him a 4+ invulnerable save.
 Faction Keywords Imperium, Astra Militarum, <Regiment> Keywords Character, Infantry, Officer, Lord Solar Macharius

 Abilities Anti-grav Upwash: Whilst this unit contains 3 models, models in this unit have a Move characteristic of 20". 1Ratling Chef
Name M WS BS S T W A Ld Sv Ratling Chef 5" 5+ 3+ 2 2 2 1 6 6+
A Ratling Chef is a single model equipped with a ladle, stub pistol and hot stew.
Weapon Range Type S AP D Abilities Ladle Melee Melee +1 - 1 - Stub Pistol 9" Pistol 1 4 0 1 - Abilities
Shoot Sharp and Scarper: Immediately after making a Shooting attack (other than firing Overwatch), this unit can move as if it were the Movement phase (though it cannot Advance as part of this move).
Naturally Stealthy: Models in this unit receive a +2 bonus to their saving throw when they receive the benefits of cover instead of only +1.
 Hot Stew: Instead of firing Overwatch, this model can hurl his stew. Roll a D6: on a 4+ the charging unit takes 1 mortal wound. In addition, Ratling units within 6" of a friendly Ratling Chef can use the Chef's Leadership instead of their own. Faction Keywords Imperium, Astra Militarum, Militarum Auxilia Keywords Character, Infantry, Ratling, Ratling Chef

Ratling Cook appears among the Adventurers on Stuff of Legends.
